About the International Relations Office

Our Strategic Goals

The International Relations Office (IRO) is dedicated to advancing PU's global engagement through strategic initiatives that foster academic excellence, research collaboration, and cultural diversity.

Strategic Partnerships

Develop and sustain strategic international partnerships with universities, research institutions, and organizations worldwide.

Academic Exchange

Promote academic, research, and cultural exchange programs for students, faculty, and staff.

Global Visibility

Enhance PU's international visibility and improve its performance in global rankings and accreditations.
